Chipmunk

One chipmunk can gather up to 165 acorns in a day.

  • Scientific Name: Tamias striatus
  • Habitat: North America & Asia
  • Life Span: 3 years
  • Diet: Omnivore

Chipmunks are part of the squirrel family. These small mammals fulfill several important functions in forest ecosystems. Their activities harvesting and hoarding tree seeds play a crucial role in seedling establishment.
